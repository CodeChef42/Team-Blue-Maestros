Overview

CrisisGuard is an adaptive, AI-powered ransomware shield that works completely offline — no cloud dependencies or external servers. It monitors your files, processes, and behaviors in real time to detect anomalies before encryption occurs.

During global cyber incidents or heightened phishing waves, CrisisGuard automatically enters Crisis Mode, tightening thresholds and increasing backup frequency to ensure maximum resilience.

With features like local AI detection, AES-GCM encrypted backups, and dynamic confidence scoring, CrisisGuard empowers users with full control, transparency, and uncompromised data sovereignty.

Why It Matters

Ransomware poses a serious and direct risk to individuals and families even though it is generally portrayed through big corporate attacks. To an individual, being a victim means that they will lose access to those digital memories that cannot be replaced such as family photos, personal documents, and financial records, most of which are kept only on a home computer or personal cloud. The force of ransom payment is terrible since, unlike a corporation that can retrieve data from a secure, professionally managed backup, the average individual has little to no reliable, segregated recovery plan. The disparity in preparedness is what gives the criminals the audacity to go after home users, ask for ransoms, and make the attack a very personal and often devastating form of digital theft.
